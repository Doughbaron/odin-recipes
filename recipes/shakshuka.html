Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a dish with tomatoes and eggs. Serve it with bread or another starch I guess.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned Tomato

Directions

  1. Sautee the onion in olive oil. Just before onion is browned, add the garlic.
  2. Add the tomatoes and sautee until raw flavor is cooked out.
  3. Add several eggs into dimples and cook until whites are no longer clear.
